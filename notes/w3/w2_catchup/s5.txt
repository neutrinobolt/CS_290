Tables

    <table>
        <tr> [table row (vertical)]
            <th> [table head] (Goes above respective column)
            </th>
            <td colspan/rowspan = [int] [makes data cell that many columns wide/long. Optional]> [table data cell]
            </td>
        </tr>
        <caption>
        [Text for caption (goes above table)]
        </caption>
    </table>
