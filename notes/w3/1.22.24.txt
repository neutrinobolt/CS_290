CSS
    Background Image
    Syntax:
        body {
            background-image: url("[image_link]"); [choose image]
            background-repeat: [set the way image repeats]
        }
    
    !important:
        Adds more importance to a property than normal
        Stick it onto the end of a property
        Overrides any other definitions
        DO NOT USE

    Float: stick something somewhere on page
    Clear: Empty space to side of object of floated items
